Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices & seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Step by Step Instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

Layering the Lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat layers, then top with remaining Parmesan
  8. Cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees Fahrenheit for 50 minutes
