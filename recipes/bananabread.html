Banana Bread Recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Bananas

Step by Step Instructions

  1. Combine dry ingredients in a bowl
  2. Beat the butter and sugar in another bowl
  3. Add eggs and mashed bananas into butter and sugar bowl
  4. Add wet mixture to dry mixture
  5. Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake in a preheated oven
  6. Bake in a 350 degree Fahrenheit oven for an hour
